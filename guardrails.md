# Guardrails Implementation

## Objective

To prevent AI hallucination and ensure reliable outputs

---

## Guardrails Used

### 1. Deterministic Logic

* Fixed if-else structure
* No randomness

---

### 2. Controlled Inputs

* Only "yes/no" responses allowed
* Limited reasoning options

---

### 3. No Open-ended Generation

* AI does not generate free text
* Outputs are predefined

---

### 4. Transparent Decision Flow

* Each output traceable to input
* No black-box reasoning

---

### 5. Actionable Outputs

* Every negative branch gives improvement action

---

## Result

* Predictable system
* No hallucination
* High reliability

---

## Key Learning

> AI becomes powerful when combined with structured systems, not when used blindly.
